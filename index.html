The Fixer. The Absorber. The one who holds the world together in public, only to quietly collapse the moment the door shuts at night. I know that person, because for twenty years, I was them.

At thirteen, while most kids were worried about school, I was managing a household fractured by divorce and trauma. As the oldest of three, my brother and sister weren't just my siblings — they were my priorities.

By 21, I realized the hard truth: my strength was actually enabling the chaos, destroying my relationships, and erasing my own life. Statistically, I wasn't supposed to make it. But I didn't just survive. I found a way out of the cycle.

Reclaim Coaching was forged where theory ends and reality begins: in the heart of a family that didn't know how to heal. I built the Reclaim Framework to save myself, and the results were life-changing. By leaning into forgiveness and grief, I repaired my family bonds and unlocked a level of professional and personal growth I never thought possible. Today, that framework is the foundation of my life and I'm here to help it become yours, too.
